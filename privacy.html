Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 22, 2025

Markit Media Group ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data in compliance with Indian laws, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA), Information Technology Act, 2000, and IT (Intermediary Guidelines and Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other identifiers provided when you contact us or engage with our services.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and browsing behavior on our website.
  • Social Media Data: Information from social media interactions, as permitted by platform policies and your consent.

2. How We Collect Data

We collect data through:

  • Direct interactions (e.g., forms, inquiries).
  • Automated technologies (e.g., cookies, analytics tools) with your prior consent where required.
  • Third-party sources (e.g., social media platforms) in compliance with applicable laws.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

We use your data to:

  • Provide digital marketing and content creation services.
  • Improve our website and user experience.
  • Communicate with you (e.g., respond to queries, send promotional materials with consent).
  • Comply with legal obligations under Indian laws.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: Obtained explicitly before collecting sensitive personal data (per DPDPA 2023).
  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill services you’ve requested.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Indian regulations (e.g., IT Rules 2021).

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Government authorities, if required by law (e.g., under Section 69 of the IT Act, 2000).
  • Social media platforms for marketing purposes, with your consent.

We do not sell your personal data.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures as mandated by the IT Act, 2000 (Section 43A) and DPDPA 2023 to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Under the DPDPA 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request details of your data we hold.
  • Correction: Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of data processing where consent is the basis.

To exercise these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@markitmedia.in.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect functionality. Consent for non-essential cookies is obtained as per IT Rules 2021.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law (e.g., tax or audit requirements). Post-retention, data is securely deleted.

10. International Data Transfers

If data is transferred outside India (e.g., to cloud servers), we ensure compliance with DPDPA 2023 and applicable safeguards under Section 75 of the IT Act, 2000.

  • Response Time: Within 30 days as per DPDPA 2023
